Leitir.is – public libraries

You are located in the public libraries section of leitir.is. Here you are searching both physical and electronic materials in the public libraries in Iceland. Leitir.is provides information on books, journals and articles, e-books, audio books, music, theses, maps, visual materials, photographs and artifacts. It includes holdings from Icelandic libraries.
